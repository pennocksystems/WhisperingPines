Whispering Pines Golf Club

18-Hole Championship Course Overview

Hole 1 – Pine Gateway

Par: 4 | Distance: 375 yds

A welcoming dogleg right with tall pines lining the fairway—stay center-left off the tee for a clear approach.

Hole 2 – Sandtrap Serenade

Par: 3 | Distance: 160 yds

A short but tricky par 3 with two guarding bunkers—precision is key.

Hole 3 – Whispering Fairway

Par: 5 | Distance: 530 yds

A long par 5 with a scenic downhill drive and elevated green surrounded by native grasses.

Hole 18 – Pines Finale

Par: 5 | Distance: 545 yds

A dramatic finishing hole with a wide fairway leading to a guarded green—finish strong!
