Whispering Pines Golf Club

A Legacy Rooted in Nature

Our Founder – Harold “Hal” Whitmore

Established: 1963

Whispering Pines Golf Club was born from a dream — Hal Whitmore’s dream. A lifelong golfer and forestry conservationist, Hal envisioned a course where the harmony of sport and nature could be truly felt.

In the spring of 1963, Hal broke ground on a sprawling 200-acre stretch of Southern woodland. With nothing more than a passion for the game and a crew of loyal locals, Hal designed and built the first nine holes himself. The course quickly became a regional treasure, later expanding to 18 holes and earning acclaim for its peaceful ambiance and thoughtful layout.

The name “Whispering Pines” came from Hal’s favorite afternoon tradition — sitting on the porch of the original clubhouse, listening to the gentle breeze whisper through the towering longleaf pines. It was here he would often say, “Let the wind guide your swing and the trees calm your mind.”

Over six decades, Whispering Pines has hosted local legends, charity classics, and generations of weekend warriors. Today, Hal’s legacy lives on in every fairway curve, every shaded tee box, and every whisper in the trees.
